Q: I got married in my home country and later obtained Spanish nationality. Can I sponsor my spouse for family reunification?

A: Yes, but first, it is necessary to register the marriage that took place abroad with the Central Civil Registry in Spain and obtain the Family Book (Libro de Familia).
